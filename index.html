How it Works

To use the ingredient comparer, simply type the name of two skincare products in the boxes and hit enter. This program will retrieve the ingredient list for each product, and conduct an analysis on both, comparing them and informing you if any pairings of products could be damaging to your skin based on the chemical composition of the product.

My Mission

I was really uneducated on what to use for acne, and I kinda just went with whatever had good reviews. I ended up using products that had Retinol or Benzoyl Peroxide, which when mixed irritates your skin. Don't be a dumbass like me. I created this website as a resource for people to make smarter skincare decisions. Combining certain ingredients can reduce their effectiveness, amplify skin irritation, or destabilize key ingredients. It's important to know what works with each other—and what doesn’t.
